# Known Issues (Beta)

- Workday forms vary heavily across companies and can change dynamically; some pages will have lower field detection accuracy.
- Some fields may require manual review or manual entry by design (sensitive or uncertain fields are not auto-filled).
- The extension does not submit applications. You must personally submit on the job site.
- CAPTCHA/security challenge pages are not supported (no bypassing or workarounds).
- AI outputs (cover letters/suggested answers) should always be reviewed and edited for accuracy before use.
