# Demo Script (Beta Launch)

Messaging to keep consistent:
- “AI job application copilot”
- “review-first autofill”
- “user-controlled submission”
- “no auto-submit, no fabricated experience, no CAPTCHA/login bypassing”

## 60-Second Demo Flow (Founder Live Demo)
1. Open the web app homepage:
   - Show the promise: autofill + tailored cover letters + tracker, while you stay in control.
2. Sign up / sign in:
   - Go to `/signup` (invite-only: paste invite code).
3. Upload resume:
   - Go to `/onboarding` and upload a resume.
   - Mention: “We extract facts and you approve them before anything is used.”
4. Open a job application page:
   - Greenhouse / Lever / Workday page works best for demo.
5. Open the extension side panel:
   - Sign in from the extension if needed.
6. Detect job + scan fields:
   - Click “Detect job + scan fields”
7. Suggest answers:
   - Click “Suggest answers”
   - Call out: “Sensitive/uncertain fields are left blank for you to answer.”
8. Review-first autofill:
   - Click “Fill application”
   - Show warning box if any fields were unsupported.
9. User-controlled submission:
   - Point at the submit button on the site and say:
     - “You personally submit. The extension never clicks submit.”
10. Record application + export:
  - Click “Record application”
  - Export tracker to `.xlsx` (from web or extension)

## TikTok Demo Flow (Fast + Visual)
Goal: show “forms are the worst” → “we remove the repetition” → “you stay in control”.

Shot list (15–25 seconds):
1. Quick clip of a long ATS form.
2. Open extension side panel:
   - “Detect fields”
3. “Suggest answers”:
   - show “needs your approval” lines
4. “Fill application”:
   - show fields filling quickly
5. End frame:
   - “Review-first autofill. You submit.”
   - “Tracks everything. Export to Excel.”

On-screen text to avoid:
- “auto-apply”
- “bot”
- “mass apply”

## LinkedIn Launch Post Flow
Post structure:
1. Problem:
   - “Applying isn’t hard, it’s repetitive.”
2. What we built:
   - “An AI job application copilot that helps you fill applications faster.”
3. Trust angle:
   - “Review-first autofill. User-controlled submission.”
   - “No auto-submit. No fabricated experience. No CAPTCHA bypassing.”
4. What it does:
   - Autofill approved fields
   - Tailored cover letters from your real experience only
   - Track every application + Excel export
5. Beta call-to-action:
   - “DM me for an invite” or “Join the waitlist”

## Reddit Launch Explanation (r/cscareerquestions style)
Tone: transparent and safety-first.

Template:
- “I built a job application copilot to reduce repetitive form-filling.”
- “It’s not an auto-apply tool. It won’t submit for you.”
- “It doesn’t bypass CAPTCHAs/logins, and it won’t invent experience.”
- “It fills only approved, non-sensitive fields after you review.”
- “If you want to try it, I’m running a small invite-only beta.”

Be ready to answer:
- What permissions does the extension request and why?
- What data is stored?
- Can users delete their data?
- Does it work on Workday/Greenhouse/Lever?

## Key Product Messaging (One-Liners)
- “Autofill the repetitive parts, keep the human in control.”
- “Review-first autofill with auditability.”
- “Faster applications without compromising credibility.”
